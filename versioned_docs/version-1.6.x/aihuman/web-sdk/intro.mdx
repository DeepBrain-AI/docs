**AI Human SDK** is a powerful developer toolkit that enables you to build services using AI models trained via deep learning, delivered in **TTV (Text to Video)** format, and capable of natural voice-based interaction, just like a real human.

The core component of the SDK, <Highlight color="#5548FF">**AIPlayer**</Highlight>, renders an AI avatar on screen in real time and provides it as a view component that can be freely positioned within your application. 
These AI Avatars are built by learning actual human **voices and facial expressions**, resulting in a **much more lifelike and engaging experience** compared to traditional, robotic TTS (Text to Speech) systems.

### Key Features
- **Real-Time Interaction**  
Like a video call, the AI can engage in smooth, natural conversations and display a wide range of **gestures and facial expressions** in real time.

- **Automatic State Management**  
Once initialized, the AI enters an **Idle** state, performing subtle actions such as blinking or nodding—creating a **realistic human-like presence**.

- **Flexible Customization**
  + Select different AI Avatars
  + Control speech and gestures
  + Adjust size, position, and speaking speed
  + Support for pause, resume, and stop functions

- **Multilingual Support**  
Offers AI Avatars in **Korean, English, Japanese, Chinese**, and more—enabling seamless deployment of **multilingual, global services**.

**AI Human SDK** is designed to help developers effortlessly integrate **interactive, human-like AI Avatars** into web applications. With minimal setup, you can deliver an immersive, next-generation AI experience.
